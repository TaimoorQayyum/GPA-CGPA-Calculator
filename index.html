How to Calculate GPA

GPA is calculated using the following formula:

GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credit Hours

Where:

  • Grade Points for a course = Credit Hours × Grade Point Value
  • Total Grade Points = Sum of all Grade Points
  • Total Credit Hours = Sum of all Credit Hours

How to Calculate CGPA

CGPA (Cumulative GPA) is calculated using the following formula:

CGPA = Sum(Semester GPA × Semester Credit Hours) ÷ Total Credit Hours

This calculator automatically performs these calculations for you based on the data you enter.
